POLICY ADVISORY & COLLABORATION

I engage in policy advisory and research-based collaboration in the field of European defence industrial policy, European security, and EU–NATO relations. My work focuses on the intersection between law, industrial strategy, and geopolitical transformation, with particular attention to how European defence capabilities are structured, financed, and governed.

My research and advisory activity is grounded in EU law and security governance, with a specific focus on the evolution of the European defence industrial base, the legal framework of the Common Security and Defence Policy (CSDP), and the regulatory and institutional dimensions of EU–NATO cooperation.

I have previously served as a policy advisor at the Italian Permanent Representation to NATO, where I worked on defence and security policy issues within the Alliance framework. This experience informs my understanding of how institutional decision-making and strategic industrial considerations interact in practice.

I am currently engaged with policy-oriented research networks and think tanks working on defence and security issues, and I am a member of the pool of experts of Quadrant Strategies. My engagement in this context focuses on providing analytical input on European defence industrial policy, transatlantic security coordination, and related geopolitical developments.

My advisory and collaborative work is primarily focused on:

  • European defence industrial policy and capability development
  • EU–NATO cooperation and institutional coordination
  • Security implications of industrial and fiscal policy in Europe
  • Legal and regulatory dimensions of defence integration
  • Geopolitical risk and European strategic autonomy

I am open to collaboration with think tanks, research institutes, and international organisations working at the intersection of law, defence policy, and industrial strategy.
