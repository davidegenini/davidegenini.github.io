About

Davide Genini is a PhD candidate in EU law and policy at Dublin City University and a member of the Dublin European Law Institute. In 2025, he was awarded the prestigious Irish Research Council Fellowship, and in 2026, he is a visiting fellow at the Luxembourg Centre of European Law. His research focuses on European security law, EU–NATO relations, and EU integration. He has published extensively in peer-reviewed journals, including the Oxford Yearbook of European Law, European Foreign Affairs Review, Legal Issues of Economic Integration, and the Maastricht Journal of European and Comparative Law. He has also provided commentary on European law and politics in outlets such as EJIL:Talk!, the Columbia Journal of Transnational Law, and The Loop.

Davide is involved in cross-border projects, including ALCIDE (Activating the Law Creatively to Integrate Defence in Europe) and the PROSPER Jean Monnet Network. He previously served as a policy advisor at the Italian Permanent Representation to NATO and as a researcher in EU law at Bocconi University.
